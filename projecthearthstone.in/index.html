The Problem

What if your voice could not be heard?

Communication is a basic human need — yet every day, millions of people struggle to be heard. Not because they have nothing to say, but because society cannot understand their language.

There are nearly 70 million deaf and non-verbal individuals worldwide. In emergencies, classrooms, hospitals, and public spaces, communication barriers can become dangerous and socially ostracizing.

Silence should never mean invisibility. That is why Project Hearthstone exists.

About Us

What is
Project Hearthstone?

Project Hearthstone is a student-led initiative building technology that bridges the gap between the hearing and the deaf community. We believe that access to communication tools should be universal—not a privilege.

Through AI-powered sign language recognition, interactive learning tools, and accessible product design, we are working to make the world more inclusive one interaction at a time.

Our Purpose

01

Mission

To empower deaf, hard-of-hearing, and non-verbal individuals by creating accessible, affordable, and intuitive communication technology—bridging the gap between those who sign and those who don't.

"Technology should remove barriers, not create them."
02

Vision

A world where communication barriers no longer define a person's opportunities. Where technology seamlessly translates intent across languages—verbal, signed, or otherwise—in real time.

"Every person deserves to be understood, in any language."
